Our goal is to provide German learners with a training App.
It all started in Germany when we were studying German in order to pass the merciless Studienkolleg German test. From that experience we noticed that it was a hassle training for the exams with the little books that we were given. So, from that experience came out the following requirements for the App:

  • Tests are available for any kind of vocabulary.
  • Repeating tests should be easy.
  • I want to know how far I am with the learning progress!
